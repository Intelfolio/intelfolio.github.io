Intelfolio’s dashboard enables you to benchmark your company against your closest peers and competitors in a timely fashion. Have all of the information you need to drive your business forward at your fingertips.

Ensure you are focusing on what matters most to help your business thrive. Get access to KPIs and benchmarks tailored specifically to your industry, segment and location.

You will receive live and user-friendly reports every reporting period. We cover 2,500 segments within 1,500 industries with the number of participants growing daily.

Data Entry

Intelfolio is based on an insights-for-insights model—you gain access to your competitors’ data if you share your data. We keep the identity of your company and all other companies that share information with us confidential. Subscription members can only see a company’s metrics within Intelfolio—not the company’s name.
